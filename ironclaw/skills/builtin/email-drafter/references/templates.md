# Email Templates Reference

## Meeting Request

**Subject:** Meeting request — [Topic] — [Date options]

Hi [Name],

I'd like to schedule a [30/60]-minute meeting to discuss [specific topic]. Would any of the following times work for you?

- [Option 1: Day, Date, Time + timezone]
- [Option 2]
- [Option 3]

If none of these work, please suggest a time that suits you.

[What we'll cover / why it's important in 1-2 sentences]

Best,
[Your name]

---

## Follow-up (no response)

**Subject:** Following up — [Original subject]

Hi [Name],

I wanted to follow up on my email from [date/last week]. I understand you're busy — just checking whether you had a chance to look at [specific thing].

[One-sentence reminder of the ask or context]

Please let me know if you need any additional information. Happy to chat briefly if that's easier.

Best,
[Your name]

---

## Introduction

**Subject:** Introduction — [Your name] / [Connection's name]

Hi [Name],

I'd like to introduce you to [Person], [title/role] at [Company]. [One sentence on why this introduction is valuable.]

[Person], meet [Name]. [One sentence on why this person is relevant to them.]

I'll leave it to you both to take it from here. I hope this is helpful!

Best,
[Your name]

---

## Rejection / Decline

**Subject:** Re: [Original subject]

Hi [Name],

Thank you for reaching out / for your proposal / for your application.

After careful consideration, we've decided not to move forward at this time [because / as we're currently focused on X — optional].

I appreciate your [time / interest / effort] and wish you all the best.

Best regards,
[Your name]

---

## Apology / Service Recovery

**Subject:** Apology regarding [specific issue]

Hi [Name],

I want to sincerely apologise for [specific problem]. This is not the standard we hold ourselves to, and I understand how frustrating this must have been.

Here is what happened: [brief, factual explanation — no excuses]

Here is what we are doing to fix it: [concrete action + timeline]

[Optional: compensation or goodwill gesture]

Please don't hesitate to reach out if you have any further concerns.

Sincerely,
[Your name]
